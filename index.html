Common Questions

Is this still homeschooling?

Your child learns from home on a flexible schedule. The difference: certified teachers run the instruction, and the program is formally accredited. Many families see it as "homeschool with professional support."

Can I enroll mid-year?

Yes. Legacy accepts students any time of year. We'll assess where your child is and place them at the right level. No waiting for September.

What about socialization?

Students interact in live classes with peers from 30+ countries. There are group projects, discussions, and virtual clubs. Plus, your child still has time for local activities, sports, and co-ops.

How does the MCSF scholarship work?

The Maine Children's Scholarship Fund provides up to $900/year for families who qualify by income. Applications are open January through April. Legacy is an eligible school. The scholarship covers roughly 50% of annual tuition.

What if my child is ahead or behind grade level?

We assess each student individually. If your 5th grader does 7th-grade math, we place them there. If they need support in reading, we meet them where they are. Flexible pacing is one of the main reasons families choose us.

Do colleges recognize this diploma?

Yes. Legacy is accredited by WASC and Cognia. Our transcripts and diplomas are accepted by universities across the US and internationally. We provide the same documentation as any traditional accredited school.
